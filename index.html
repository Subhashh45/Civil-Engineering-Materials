About

Civil engineering materials are essential for the planning, design, and construction of infrastructure. The selection of appropriate materials affects the strength, durability, and sustainability of structures.

Products / Materials

  • Concrete: A composite material made of cement, sand, and gravel. Widely used for buildings, roads, and bridges.
  • Steel: Known for its high tensile strength. Often used in reinforced concrete and structural frameworks.
  • Bricks: Traditional building material made from clay. Common in residential and low-rise buildings.
  • Asphalt: Used in road construction for flexible pavement structures.
  • Timber: Renewable and lightweight, suitable for housing and interior design.
